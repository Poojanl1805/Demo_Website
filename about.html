A HOME is a luxury.No matter the price.

Candiclie Constructions is a prominent real estate development company known for its commitment to crafting exceptional living spaces. Established with a visionary zeal in 2023 by Ravi Singh, the company has since evolved into a trusted name in the industry. With a focus on redefining the real estate landscape, Candiclie Constructions endeavors to create communities where individuals can thrive. Their portfolio boasts successful projects across major cities like Chennai, Coimbatore, Bangalore, Mumbai, Erode, Salem, Tuticorin, and beyond. Guided by values of integrity, innovation, and excellence, the company is dedicated to connecting people with their dream homes while fostering lasting value for generations to come. Through transparent practices and a customer-centric approach, Candiclie Constructions continues to set new standards of quality and reliability in the real estate sector.
